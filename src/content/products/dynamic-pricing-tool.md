Manual pricing is leaving money on the table. We resisted dynamic pricing tools for the first year of hosting, convinced we knew our market well enough to set rates ourselves. We were wrong — within the first month of switching to automated pricing, revenue jumped noticeably and occupancy stayed steady.

## How It Works

Dynamic pricing tools analyze market data including local events, competitor rates, seasonal demand, day-of-week patterns, and booking lead times to adjust your nightly rate automatically. Most tools update prices daily and can be configured with minimum and maximum rate floors so you never go below what you're comfortable with.

## The Results

In our case, the tool identified patterns we'd missed entirely. Wednesday nights in summer were being underpriced by 30%. Holiday weekends needed to be listed higher and earlier. Last-minute gaps could be filled with strategic discounts rather than sitting empty.
