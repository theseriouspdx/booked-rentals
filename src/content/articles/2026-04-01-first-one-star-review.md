It's the notification every host dreads. You wake up, check your phone, and there it is — one star. Not three. Not two. One. And the review reads like the guest stayed at a completely different property than the one you've spent months perfecting.

Take a breath. This happens to every single host eventually, including Superhosts with hundreds of five-star reviews. The difference between hosts who recover and hosts who spiral isn't the review itself — it's how they respond in the 48 hours that follow.

## Don't Respond Immediately

Your first instinct will be to fire back a detailed rebuttal. Resist that urge. Anything you write in the first hour will be driven by emotion, and public responses live forever. Give yourself at least 24 hours before drafting a reply. In the meantime, screenshot the review and save any relevant message threads with the guest — you may need them if you decide to pursue removal through the platform.

## Assess Whether It's Removable

Not all one-star reviews are legitimate. If the review contains factually incorrect statements, references events that didn't happen, or violates the platform's review policy (like retaliatory reviews tied to a rules enforcement), you have grounds for a removal request. Document everything and submit your case through the resolution center with specific policy references, not just "this isn't fair."

## Craft Your Public Response

If the review stays, your public response matters more than the review itself. Future guests will read both, and they're evaluating your professionalism, not the complaint. Keep it short, acknowledge any legitimate concerns, explain what you've done to address them, and move on. Never get personal, never get defensive, and never argue point by point. The goal is to show future guests that you're a reasonable, responsive host.

## Bury It With Excellence

One bad review in a sea of great ones is a blip. The fastest path back to confidence is your next five-star stay. Focus your energy forward — refine your check-in process, add a small welcome touch, follow up with a friendly message after checkout. The math is on your side: one bad review among fifty great ones barely moves the needle.
